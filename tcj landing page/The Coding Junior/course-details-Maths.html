Topics to be covered

Below mentioned are the topics that are covered in this course

  • Ages, Alligations, Averages, Chain rule, Divisibility, LCM & HCF, Numbers Number System, Partnership, Percentages, Profit & loss, Ratio & proportion, Remainders
  • Integers and its operations (including exponentiation), and comparison
  • Basic modular arithmetic: addition, subtraction, and multiplication
  • Prime numbers
  • Fractions and percentages
  • Line, line segment, angle, triangle, rectangle, square, and circle
  • The point, vector, and coordinates in the plane
  • Polygon (vertex, side/edge, simple, convex, inside, and area)
  • Euclidean distances
  • Pythagorean theorem
  • Geometry in 3D
  • Modular division and inverse elements
  • Complex numbers
  • Number sequence completion
  • Number theory
  • Arithmetic progression, geometric progression, harmonic progression

Couse Objective

  • Students will evaluate limits using algebraic, geometric, analytic techniques.
  • Students will demonstrate proficiency in algebra.
  • Students enjoy mathematics and develop patience and persistence when solving problems
  • They become confident in using mathematics to analyse and solve problems both in school and in real-life situations
  • They develop the knowledge, skills and attitudes necessary to pursue further studies in mathematics
  • They develop abstract, logical and critical thinking
  • They develop a critical appreciation of the use of information and communication technology in mathematics
  • It helps in gaining knowledge and understanding

Course Outcome

  1. Solve open-ended elementary school problems in using visualization and statistical reasoning
  2. Identify, explain, and evaluate geometry, probability and statistics.
  3. Inculcate mathematical reasoning.
