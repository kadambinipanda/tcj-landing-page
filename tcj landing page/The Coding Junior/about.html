About Us

The Coding Junior is one of the upcoming ventures in the world of coding nurturing. It is a leading child institute for self-empowerment. Having its office in Kanpur - the commercial hub of Uttar Pradesh and home to a leading technical institute - the IIT Kanpur, TCJ The Coding Junior has been the brainchild of young Tech Graduates who wanted to take coding essentials learning to be imparted the way it should be with a focus on coding essentials, first.

THE CODING JUNIOR

Today's routine schooling leaves your child getting into the vicious cycle of going to school and staying at home too tired to work on other curricular activities, leaving little time to focus on creative developmental conventional activities like - painting/dancing to future enabled coding exercises.

  • The Coding Junior introduces and prepares your kids between the age group of 8-20 years to prepare for the new World order.
  • They learn fundamentals of coding - logic, structure, sequence and algorithmic thinking - to generate creative outcomes like animations and apps.
  • Our classes take care to teach live 1:1 Online in the convenience of your home by Top percentile of teachers and subject matter experts.
